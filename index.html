Frequently Asked Questions

Things you might be wondering... with helpful answers.

Is Collatable free?

Collatable comes with a free trial for 30 days. After that to continue to collate data from across your organization you'll need to select a paid subscription plan.

Can Collatable run on my corporate network?

Right now Collatable runs only within the cloud, however there are solutions available to allow Collatable to work with data in your organization that isn't exposed to the internet.

Does Collatable work with data in SQL databases?

Yes, absolutely! Collatable can connect to over 50 data sources including SQL databases. The data will be kept up to date and pushed back to the SQL database after review.

What if my team don't update the records?

Collatable has built in reporting and analytics to surface any missing updates that are pending from your team. It also automatically reminds those required to provide updates.
